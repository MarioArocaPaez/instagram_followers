How to Use InstaBack

To get started with InstaBack, follow these simple steps. First, download your Instagram data by going to your Instagram settings and selecting "Download your information." Request a copy of your data and choose to download "Part of your information." Select only "Followers and followed," and set the date range to "Since the beginning." Choose "JSON" as the format and set the quality to "Medium." You will receive a .zip file that includes your following.json and followers_1.json files. These files are necessary to check who is not following you back.

Once you have downloaded the data, extract the files and upload them here. Use the form above to upload the following.json and followers_1.json files, then click the "Check" button to see the results.

Unlike typical apps that require access to your Instagram account, InstaBack is different. We don't need access to your account; we only need your help in getting the relevant data from Instagram. Once you provide the necessary files, we will process them and give you the results. Rest assured, InstaBack does not store any data. The files you provide and the results generated are used only for the purpose of providing you with the information you requested, and nothing is stored or saved.
