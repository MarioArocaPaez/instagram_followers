How to Use InstaBack

To get started with InstaBack, follow these detailed steps to download and use your Instagram data. This process will help you identify who isn't following you back on Instagram.

1. Open the Instagram app and tap on the three horizontal lines (menu) in the top-right corner of your profile page.

2. Navigate to the “Settings” menu by scrolling down and selecting “Settings”.

3. In the settings menu, find and select “Download Your Information” to start the process of downloading your data.

4. Choose the option “Download or transfer your information” to proceed with data export.

5. When prompted, select “Some of your information” to specify the types of data you want to download.

6. Scroll down to the “Connections” section and check the box next to “Followers and following”. Click on “Next” to continue.

7. Choose “Download to device” to save the files directly to your device.

8. Set the “Date range” option to “All time” to include all followers and following data since you started using Instagram.

9. Set the “Format” to “JSON” as it is the required format for InstaBack to process the data.

10. Set the “Media quality” to “Medium” to ensure the data is manageable in size and suitable for the required analysis.

11. After setting all the options, click on “Create files” to generate your data request.

12. Instagram will process your request, which may take a few minutes. You will receive a notification email once the data is ready for download.

13. After receiving the notification, return to the “Download Your Information” section in Instagram, scroll down to the pending request, and choose “Download”.

14. You will be prompted to enter your Instagram password to confirm the download. Once entered, the data will be downloaded as a .zip file.

15. Unzip the downloaded file to access its contents. If you are unsure how to unzip files on Android or iOS, please refer to Unzip files in Android or Unzip files in iOS for guidance.

16. Go to the InstaBack website and use the form provided to upload the necessary files. Navigate to the “connections/followers_and_following” folder in the unzipped data and select the following.json and followers_1.json files.

17. After selecting the files, click the "Check" button. InstaBack will process the data and display a list of users who are not following you back, along with their usernames, Instagram profile links, and the date you started following them.

18. If desired, you can save the results as a PDF for future reference.

Unlike typical apps that require access to your Instagram account, InstaBack is different. We don't need access to your account; we only need your help in getting the relevant data from Instagram. Once you provide the necessary files, we will process them and give you the results. Rest assured, InstaBack does not store any data. The files you provide and the results generated are used only for the purpose of providing you with the information you requested, and nothing is stored or saved.
