How to Use InstaBack

To get started, download your Instagram data by going to your Instagram settings and selecting "Download your information." Choose "JSON" format and download your followers data. Extract the files, then upload following.json and followers_1.json using the form above.

InstaBack processes the files you upload and checks who doesn't follow you back. Your data is not stored or saved by InstaBack. We only use it to show you the information you requested.
